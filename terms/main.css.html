Spotistats for Spotify

Free

Don't feel like waiting until the end of the year to see your Spotify Wrapped? No problem, you can do that any time of the year with up-to-date data!

Different time ranges

All stats in the app can be checked in 3 different time ranges: 4 weeks, 6 months and lifetime so you can seamlessly see when you were into what music.

Top tracks & artists

Check which tracks and artists you streamed the most. Calculated with machine learning based on, but not limited to, how often you streamed the song and when.

Privacy

A single login through the Spotify app unlocks all the features to you in the app. All your data is stored locally on your device and your device only. Privacy is key.

Advanced song insights

See per track, album or playlist advanced audio analyses. Are your tracks energetic or sad? Let's see!

Create playlists

Let us create a playlist of your top 50 tracks, we will also sync it automatically while we are at it!

Listening history

Check your full listening history. See how often you've streamed a track and when you've streamed it. Calculate graphs of what your most active hours are, and check how many minutes you've listened in total.
